Yeah! biography

Yeah! was released on January 10, 2004 as the lead single for Usher's fourth studio album Confessions. It was recorded in late 2003, when the label listened to the album and felt none of the tracks waranted being a strong lead single. When it came out, the song spent 12 consecutive weeks at #1 on the US Billboard Hot 100 Chart, and it was the longest running song at #1 of 2004.
